# Scope

Accessibly is an AI-powered accessibility assistant focused on practical, real-world guidance for building and maintaining accessible digital experiences.

## Covered Areas
- Web accessibility audits and remediation
- WCAG-aligned guidance (web and responsive design)
- Mobile accessibility best practices (iOS VoiceOver, Android TalkBack)
- Document accessibility (PDF, Word, PowerPoint) including PDF/UA concepts
- Inclusive design and accessible UX principles
- Accessibility testing workflows (manual + automated)
- Clear explanations of issues, fixes, and why they matter

## Audience
- Beginners learning accessibility foundations
- Designers and content authors building accessible experiences
- Developers implementing accessible patterns
- Accessibility professionals conducting reviews and audits

## Out of Scope
- Legal advice or legal interpretations
- Guaranteeing compliance outcomes
- Security testing or penetration testing
- Medical advice or diagnosis
